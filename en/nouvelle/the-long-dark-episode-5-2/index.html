The Long Dark – Episode 5

Anton
May 7, 2025

After over a decade of development, The Long Dark is set to conclude its Wintermute story mode with the highly anticipated Episode 5, titled “The Light at the End of All Things”. Scheduled for release in 2025, this episode will bring closure to the stories of Mackenzie and Astrid, while unveiling the mysteries surrounding the Aurora and the contents of the enigmatic hardcase.

An Ambitious Conclusion

According to Hinterland Studio, this episode will be their most ambitious yet, featuring two to three times more narrative and cinematic content than Episode 4.
The game world will also be three to four times larger, introducing brand-new regions to explore.

New Mechanics and Enhancements

The episode will introduce new gameplay systems, with a greater emphasis on storytelling. While the progression will be more linear than in previous episodes, players will venture into completely new areas. Additionally, significant visual enhancements, including colored shadows, ambient particle effects, and improved draw distances, will be available on PC starting in May 2025.

What’s Next?

The release of this episode will signify the completion of The Long Dark‘s original project. However, Hinterland plans to continue supporting the game with updates, including a quality-of-life update, enhanced custom settings, and mod support. Furthermore, the studio intends to consult the community to determine the future direction of the game.
